🔢 Your Pathway:

How does the Pathway system work?
Your Pathway is a set of 4 courses drawn from at least 3 different academic areas that share a theme. Think of it as a built-in interdisciplinary focus for your degree.

Pathway courses can double-dip with Breadth requirements, but a double-dipped course only counts as 3 credits toward your required 36 total distribution credits.

Some Pathway courses are labeled "Non-Breadth" — these count for your Pathway but do not count toward your 36 distribution credits or Liberal Arts total. Honors, special-topics, and study-abroad courses may count if approved.
